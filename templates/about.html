About

"Sinhala for kids" is a site dedicated to children learning Sinhala as a second language. Its primary goal is to promote skills in reading and comprehension. The games and activities presented in this site have been designed with that goal in mind.

We hope you or your child would find this site valuable.

Help

This site is maintained by volunteers and could always use any help from anyone willing to contribute. Assistance in the following areas would be most valuable:

  • Identifying and reporting any inaccuracies/mistakes in the existing activities
  • Adding content to existing activities
  • Help in improving the website design
  • Creating new games and activities
